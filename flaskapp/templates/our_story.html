Our Story

Greg Lawrence is the pioneering force behind GL Construction Services, a company that has evolved from its roots as GL Painting to a multifaceted contracting powerhouse. With a career steeped in the craftsmanship of construction, Lawrence's journey began under the mentorship of Denny, where he honed his skills and imbibed the ethos of dedication and quality workmanship.

In 2010, armed with a wealth of experience and a vision to diversify construction services, Lawrence embarked on his entrepreneurial journey, founding GL Painting. Under his leadership, the company burgeoned beyond its initial painting focus, expanding into a comprehensive array of contracting opportunities.

Embracing residential, retail, commercial, pool construction, demolitions, and specialty contracting, GL Construction Services became synonymous with innovation, excellence, and versatility in the industry. Lawrence's leadership style, marked by a relentless pursuit of perfection and an unwavering commitment to client satisfaction, has been instrumental in steering the company's growth and success.

Beyond his role as CEO, Lawrence remains deeply entrenched in the day-to-day operations of GL Construction Services, fostering a culture of teamwork, creativity, and adaptability among his employees. His forward-thinking approach and willingness to embrace new challenges have positioned the company as a trailblazer in the construction realm.

Greg Lawrence continues to drive GL Construction Services forward, continually seeking new horizons, and redefining industry standards, all while staying true to the core values of integrity, excellence, and reliability.
